Time10:00 — 13:00

This month we head into the heart of Nørrebro — one of Copenhagen's most vibrant neighbourhoods. We'll wander through Assistens Kirkegård, catch street life on Ravnsborggade, and end at Blågårds Plads for coffee and a photo chat.

Bring whatever camera you have. The vibe is relaxed, curious, and social. No critiques, no pressure — just exploring and shooting together.
