Next Walk

Date Saturday, 10 May 2025
Time 10:00 — 13:00
Meet Nørreport Station, main entrance
Route Nørrebro · Assistens Kirkegård · Blågårds Plads
Skill level All welcome — any camera, any level

This month we head into the heart of Nørrebro — one of Copenhagen's most vibrant and photogenic neighbourhoods. We'll wander through the calm of Assistens Kirkegård, catch the street life on Ravnsborggade, and end at Blågårds Plads for coffee and a photo chat.

Bring whatever camera you have. The vibe is relaxed, curious, and social. No group critiques, no pressure — just exploring and shooting together.
